Centers Under Mindful Rejuvenation

Mindful Rejuvenation operates a comprehensive network of specialized facilities across Kerala, ensuring accessible and continuous mental healthcare. This network includes two fully equipped psychiatric hospitals in Kochi and Angamaly, which offer inpatient admissions and 24/7 Clinical Monitoring, alongside our dedicated outpatient clinic in Kaloor. This strategic positioning allows us to provide expert medical consultation, evidence-based therapy, and supportive counseling to individuals and families throughout the region.
