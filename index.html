Comprehensive Mental Health Services

Complete, evidence-based mental health care for children, adults and families.

Psychiatric Consultation

Detailed clinical evaluation to understand symptoms, leading to expert guidance and individualized care planning.

Psychiatry Admissions

Structured, supportive environment with 24/7 clinical monitoring for stabilization and comprehensive initial assessment.

Behavioral Assessment

A detailed evaluation to understand behavioral patterns, triggers, and psychological factors necessary for accurate diagnosis and management.

Supportive Psychotherapy

Therapy focused on addressing current symptoms and immediate issues, enhancing coping skills, and boosting self-esteem in a safe setting.

Recovery & Impulse Control Therapy

Specialized therapy focused on identifying triggers, developing coping skills, and building emotional resilience to gain long-term control over impulsive urges.

Stress Management

Skill-building sessions to manage daily pressures, improve time management, and foster a balanced, sustainable lifestyle.

Individual Counselling

Confidential, supportive sessions with a dedicated counsellor to address personal issues, anxiety, relationship concerns, and coping skills.

Dementia Care Support

Specialized assessment and intervention focused on maximizing cognitive well-being and enhancing quality of life for individuals with dementia.

Cognitive Behavioural Therapy

Structured, evidence-based therapy focused on identifying and modifying unhelpful thought patterns to change emotional responses and behaviors.

Dialectical Behaviour Therapy

Evidence-based therapy that integrates acceptance with change strategies to help patients manage intense emotions and build a life worth living.

Telepsychiatry & Remote Consultations

Secure, remote appointments with our Psychiatrists for consultation, follow-up, and medication management from your location.

Family Therapy

Supportive sessions designed to help families understand a loved one's recovery process and build healthier, more effective interactions.
