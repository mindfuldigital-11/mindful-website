Beyond our Consultant Psychiatrists and Clinical Psychologists, a dedicated team of specialists ensures a holistic and supportive well-being environment within the hospital.

Psychiatric Social Workers (PSWs)

Our Psychiatric Social Workers are essential links between the hospital environment and the patient's external life. They specialize in psychosocial assessment, focusing on the patient's family, social, and occupational history. They provide family intervention, therapy, psychoeducation, and rehabilitation planning to support long-term well-being.

Professional Counsellors

Professional Counsellors offer supportive and skill-based sessions that focus on coping strategies, emotional regulation, stress management, personal growth, and adjustment issues — all in a confidential, supportive space.

Wellness & Rehabilitation Specialists (Yoga / Therapy)

This team supports holistic well-being through therapeutic yoga, mindfulness practices, occupational therapy, and structured recreational activities that enhance social interaction and speed up emotional and functional treatment.

Psychiatric Nursing & Clinical Support

Our Psychiatric Nursing team ensures 24/7 Clinical Monitoring, safe medication practices, emotional support, and continuous supervision. They maintain a stable therapeutic environment while coordinating closely with consultants.
